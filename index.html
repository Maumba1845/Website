About

Taro Modelmaker is an industry professional with 15 years of experience in his field. He started his buisness out of his shed and has been running it for 6 years, making and designing resin add-ons for table top war gaming. He has created his own range of busts, display plinths, scenic materials and model parts. The buisness was originally created on Etsy, where it grew to be one of the top sellers of war gaming add ons. He later moved to his own site, where he currently sells his products. He has a wide selection of products, ranging from add ons for existing models to his own busts and plinths.
